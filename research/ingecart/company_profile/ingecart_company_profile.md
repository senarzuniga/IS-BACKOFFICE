## Strategic Positioning

Ingecart is a consolidated industrial player in the printing, signage, and
visual-communication equipment market. It supplies, installs, and services
large-format printers, cutting equipment, and ancillary machinery to
professional print shops, signage workshops, agencies, and manufacturers.

### Core Competencies

1. **Equipment Supply & Integration** — large-format printers, cutters, laminators
2. **Technical Support & Maintenance** — field service network across Iberia
3. **Consumables Distribution** — inks, substrates, tools
4. **Workflow Consulting** — production optimisation for print shops
