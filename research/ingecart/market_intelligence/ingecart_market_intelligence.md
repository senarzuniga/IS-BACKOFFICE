## Dominant Trends (FESPA 2026 Edition)

1. **End-to-end production workflow automation**
   Industrial print shops are moving from manual job management to automated
   queue optimisation, driven by labour costs and margin pressure.

2. **Digitalisation of analogue processes**
   Traditional workshops with analogue workflows are the biggest greenfield
   opportunity for AI-assisted tools.

3. **Sustainability & eco-printing**
   Customer demand for eco-certified inks and substrates is rising across
   all European markets.

4. **Mass personalisation via intelligent software**
   Variable-data and short-run personalisation is becoming a commodity
   differentiator.

5. **AI in order management, quality control, and pricing** ★ HIGH GROWTH
   This is the highest-growth area with the least consolidated supply. IAR's
   agents are positioned to capture this whitespace.
