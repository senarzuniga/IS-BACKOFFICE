DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (20 MIN)

0) OPENING (1 min)
"Thanks for your time. The goal is to understand your current operation and check whether a used-machine + relocation model can fit your timeline and ROI goals."

1) CURRENT CONTEXT (4 min)
- What line/process are you currently running?
- What are your main bottlenecks today (capacity, quality, labor, downtime)?

2) URGENCY + TIMELINE (4 min)
- Why is this project relevant now?
- What commissioning date are you targeting?

3) TECHNICAL FIT (5 min)
- Required speed/format/material specs?
- Site constraints (space, utilities, shutdown window)?

4) ECONOMIC FIT (3 min)
- Budget window and approval process?
- Preferred commercial model (capex, staged payments, leasing)?

5) DECISION PATH (2 min)
- Who validates technically?
- Who signs commercially?

6) CLOSE + NEXT STEP (1 min)
"Based on this, next step is [technical dossier review / virtual walkthrough / site survey] on [date]."

MANDATORY CALL OUTPUT
- Need summary (1 paragraph)
- Qualification score (BANT+T)
- Clear next action with owner and date
