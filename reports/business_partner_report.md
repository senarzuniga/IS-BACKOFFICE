## 2. Strategy

- **Focus on High-Priority Accounts**: Engage with Oversys, Alpine Machinery, Papertek, Exapro, and Kitmondo to establish strong partnerships.
- **Leverage Marketplaces**: Use platforms like Exapro and Kitmondo for pricing benchmarks and lead generation.
- **Expand Geographic Reach**: Target North America and Europe as primary markets, with global exports as a secondary focus.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Automate data collection from marketplaces and auctions to refine pricing and demand strategies.

## 3. Channels and Tools

- **Web Forms and Direct Contact**: Utilize web forms and direct communication for initial outreach.
- **Marketplace Messaging**: Engage with buyers and sellers through marketplace platforms.
- **Automation Tools**: Implement scraping and alert systems for real-time data on listings and auctions.
- **CRM Integration**: Enrich CRM data with insights from industry associations and trade fairs.

## 4. Sectorial Numbers

- **Market Size**: The global used machinery market is valued at approximately $400 billion, with packaging and processing equipment as key segments.
- **Growth Rate**: The market is growing at a CAGR of 5%, driven by demand for cost-effective and sustainable solutions.
- **Regional Insights**: Europe and North America account for 60% of the market, with Asia emerging as a growth region.

## 5. Opportunities

- **Immediate Inventory**: Leverage Oversys' stock for quick wins in the Iberian market.
- **Corrugated Equipment**: Partner with Alpine Machinery to tap into the corrugated and converting segment.
- **Cross-Border Deals**: Utilize Papertek's expertise for international transactions.
- **Auction Dynamics**: Track platforms like Surplex and Troostwijk for distressed asset opportunities.
- **Technical Events**: Use TAPPI and similar associations to identify modernization buyers.
