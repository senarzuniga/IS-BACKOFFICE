# 03 - ICP, Prospecting Model, and Lead Scoring

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)

### ICP-1: Mid-size corrugated converter (high priority)
- 1-5 plants
- Need throughput/cost improvement
- Budget-sensitive compared with new OEM capex
- Strong need for installation and startup support

### ICP-2: Agro-food packaging operator (fruit/produce focus)
- Seasonal peaks
- Demand for robust and rapid deployment lines
- Potential fit for Ingecart's 3 ready fruit packing machines

### ICP-3: Growing independent plant with urgent capacity need
- Time-critical expansion
- Open to refurbished/used line with guarantees
- Needs financing and risk reduction

### ICP-4: Group optimizing plant network
- Relocation and redeployment opportunities
- Potential both as buyer and seller of used assets

## Lead Scoring (0-100)
- Urgency signal (0-25): expansion, replacement, closure, downtime pressure
- Budget fit (0-20): realistic range for used machine + services
- Technical fit (0-20): machine spec compatibility
- Decision access (0-20): known stakeholders reachable
- Execution feasibility (0-15): logistics/customs/project viability

Thresholds:
- 75-100: Sales Qualified Opportunity (SQO)
- 55-74: Active nurture
- <55: monitor only

## Prospecting Funnel Targets (first 12 weeks)
- 120 target accounts identified
- 300 decision-maker contacts
- 500 outbound touches
- 80 discovery interactions
- 20 qualified opportunities

## Prospecting Channels (practical stack)
1. Direct outreach to plant managers and operations directors
2. LinkedIn outreach + thought-lead content around ROI and relocation success
3. Partner introductions (brokers, integrators, logistics firms)
4. Event-driven prospecting (before and after trade fairs)
5. Trigger-based outreach (news of expansions, closures, M&A)

## Message Pillars For Prospecting
- Faster startup than new-equipment timeline
- Lower capex and measurable ROI
- End-to-end project risk reduction
- Commercial flexibility (staged milestones)

## Weekly Operating Rhythm
- Monday: update target list and trigger alerts
- Tuesday/Wednesday: outbound and follow-ups
- Thursday: discovery calls + opportunity scoring
- Friday: proposal pipeline review and next-week plan
