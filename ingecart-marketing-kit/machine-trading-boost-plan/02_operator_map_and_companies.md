## 4) Account Prioritization Logic
Classify accounts in three tiers:
- Tier A: active expansion/replacement signals + immediate decision capability
- Tier B: demand likely in 3-9 months + technical fit
- Tier C: long-term or unclear urgency

Prioritize Tier A + Tier B only in first 90 days.

## 5) Intelligence Fields Required Per Company
- Group/company name
- Plants and locations
- Current equipment base (if known)
- Active investment, closure, or relocation signals
- Decision-makers (operations, plant manager, engineering, procurement, owner)
- Likely demand type (fruit packing / FFG / relocation service / retrofit)
- Estimated budget window
- Buying horizon (0-3m, 3-9m, 9m+)

## 6) Practical Note
Do not try to map "all" players first. Build a high-confidence initial universe of 120 accounts and iterate weekly with new intelligence signals from scraping and outbound responses.
