# 04 - Outreach Playbooks (Practical Commercial Execution)

## 1) Multi-Channel Outreach Sequence (15 business days)

Day 1:
- Email 1: problem-led message + one quantified value point
- LinkedIn connection request to operations/plant stakeholder

Day 3:
- Follow-up email with machine availability and project timeline

Day 5:
- Phone call (or voice note) with clear CTA for 20-minute discovery

Day 8:
- Share technical one-pager + relocation process snapshot

Day 11:
- ROI mini-case based on buyer profile

Day 15:
- Final follow-up: "close file or schedule technical review"

## 2) Email Templates (short, practical)

### Email 1 - Initial outreach
Subject: Used [Fruit Packing / FFG] line with fast relocation support

Ingecart currently has:
- 3 fruit packing machines ready to relocate

If you are evaluating capacity increase in the next 3-9 months, we can share:
- technical dossier

- payback scenario based on your volumes

Would a 20-minute call next week be useful?

Best regards,
[Name]

### Email 2 - Follow-up with credibility
Subject: Technical dossier + relocation approach

Hello [Name],

Sharing our practical approach to reduce used-machine risk:
1) technical audit

3) dismantling + logistics planning

If you want, we can review your current line constraints and check fit in one call.

Regards,
[Name]

## 3) LinkedIn Message Templates

### Connection request
Hello [Name],
We support corrugated plants with used machine trade + relocation and startup services. I'd like to connect.

### After connection
Thanks for connecting. We currently have 3 fruit packing lines and 1 FFG available for relocation projects. If useful, I can send a short technical summary and potential ROI scenario for your operation.

## 4) Call Framework (20 minutes)
1. Current operation and bottlenecks (5 min)
2. Expansion urgency and budget window (5 min)
3. Technical fit and constraints (5 min)
4. Next step: dossier review or technical visit (5 min)

## 5) Content Strategy To Support Outbound
Publish weekly (LinkedIn + web):
- One ROI story
- One relocation/commissioning insight
- One industry signal (expansion, closure, M&A) with practical implication

## 6) Commercial Rules
- Always propose next step with date/time.
- Always attach one visual asset (machine sheet or process map).
- Never send pricing first without qualification context.
- Escalate technical specialist early when fit is promising.
