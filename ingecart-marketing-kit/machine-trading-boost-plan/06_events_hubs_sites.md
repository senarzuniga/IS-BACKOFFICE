# 06 - Sector Sites, Trade Shows, Associations, and Global Hubs

## A) Core Intelligence Sites (start here)

### Industry associations and sector bodies
- FEFCO (Europe corrugated industry)
- CCCA (Canada corrugated and containerboard)
- TAPPI / Corrugated communities (North America)
- AICC resources and events

### Trade media and news signal sources
- PaperAge (industry news and events)
- Packaging-focused trade media by region
- Investor and quarterly-result pages of major groups

### Machinery and supplier discovery
- DirectIndustry (corrugated equipment categories)
- OEM websites and product launch pages
- Marketplace listings (Exapro, Kitmondo, Machineseeker, Machinio)

## B) High-Value Events and Fairs
- SuperCorrExpo (North America corrugated ecosystem)
- drupa (global print + packaging solutions)
- SinoCorrugated / WEPACK (Asia and global buyer concentration)
- Regional packaging and converting fairs by target country

## C) Why Events Matter For Ingecart
- Fast concentration of buyers, sellers, and technical partners
- Live validation of demand themes and investment timing
- Direct access to plant-level operators and decision-makers

## D) Event Exploitation Playbook

### 8 weeks before event
- Build target list from exhibitor/attendee ecosystem
- Launch pre-event outreach: "meeting slots"

### During event
- 8-12 targeted meetings/day
- Qualify opportunities in real time using discovery checklist
- Capture competitor and pricing intelligence

### 2 weeks after event
- Follow-up campaign with technical dossier + proposed next steps
- Convert event contacts into staged opportunities

## E) Global Hubs To Prioritize
- Northern Italy / DACH machinery engineering corridor
- Iberia corrugated and agro-food packaging corridors
- Mexico and Andean packaging growth corridors
- North Africa import-dependent packaging hubs
- Eastern Europe modernization corridors

## F) Priority Scraping Targets Derived From Public Sources
- FEFCO updates/events/industry statistics pages
- drupa focus areas and exhibitor directories
- SinoCorrugated/WEPACK exhibitor and visitor channels
- SuperCorrExpo exhibitor and highlights pages

## G) Practical Rule
Treat events and hubs as pipeline accelerators, not branding-only activities.
Every event action must map to one of these outcomes:
1) new qualified account
2) active deal progression
3) partner activation for faster closing
