# 08 - 90-Day Action Plan (Execution Roadmap)

## Mission
Launch Ingecart machine-trading activity as a predictable commercial engine.

## Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Build Foundation + Activate Pipeline

### Week 1
- Finalize technical dossier for 3 fruit machines + 1 FFG
- Create sales assets (one-pagers, video walkthroughs, FAQ)
- Configure CRM fields and lead scoring

### Week 2
- Build first 120 target accounts (Tier A/B)
- Enrich with 300 contacts
- Start scraping cascade and trigger dashboard

### Week 3
- Launch outbound sequence (email + LinkedIn + calls)
- Schedule 15-20 discovery calls
- Start weekly intelligence sprint

### Week 4
- Qualify opportunities and run technical-fit reviews
- Prepare first proposals with ROI scenarios
- Identify partners for logistics/installation acceleration

## Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Qualification + Proposal Velocity

### Week 5-6
- Run technical deep-dives on top opportunities
- Conduct virtual machine walkthroughs with prospects
- Send tailored proposals with scope options

### Week 7-8
- Intensify follow-up and objection handling
- Execute partner-assisted deals where needed
- Push at least 6 opportunities into negotiation stage

## Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Close Deals + Build Repeatability

### Week 9-10
- Prioritize top 5 deals by close probability
- Commercial negotiation with milestone-based terms
- Confirm implementation windows with buyers

### Week 11-12
- Target first closures and signed LOIs
- Document deal playbooks and lessons learned
- Expand target list with second-wave accounts

## Weekly KPI Dashboard
- New accounts added
- New decision-makers identified
- Outreach touches sent
- Discovery meetings held
- Qualified opportunities
- Proposals sent
- Negotiations active
- Deals won value

## Operating Cadence and Ownership
- Sales lead: pipeline and closing motion
- Technical lead: fit validation and scope definition
- Intelligence lead: scraping, signals, prioritization
- Project lead: relocation/installation feasibility and delivery confidence

## Non-Negotiables
- No outbound without a clear ICP hypothesis
- No proposal without qualification checklist
- No forecasting without explicit next step and owner
- No deal pursuit beyond 14 days with zero progress evidence

## 90-Day Outcome Targets
- 20+ qualified opportunities
- 8+ technical validation meetings
- 4+ negotiation-stage deals
- 2+ closed transactions or signed implementation agreements
