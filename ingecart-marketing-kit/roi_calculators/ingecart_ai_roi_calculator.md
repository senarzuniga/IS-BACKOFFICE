# IAR AI Agents — ROI Calculator for Ingecart Customers

## How to Use This Model

Replace the `[INPUT]` values with your workshop's actual figures.
The model gives you a first-pass ROI estimate for adopting IAR's AI agents.

## Output: Annual ROI Calculation

### Agent 1 — Production Optimisation

```
Downtime reduction: 25% of current downtime rate
Annual revenue recovered = Machines × Hours/day × 220 days
                         × Current downtime rate × 25% reduction
                         × Revenue per hour

Example (defaults):
= 5 × 8 × 220 × 15% × 25% × 120
= EUR 19,800 / year
```

### Agent 2 — Intelligent Pricing & Quoting

```
Time saved per quote: 35 minutes (vs. 45 min current)
Monthly time saved = 80 quotes × 35 min = 2,800 min = 46.7 hours
Annual cost saving = 46.7 × 12 × EUR 35/h = EUR 19,600 / year

Additional revenue from faster response rate (est. +10% conversion):
= 80 quotes × 10% × avg. job value (EUR 500) × 12 months
= EUR 48,000 / year (upside, not guaranteed)
```

### Agent 3 — Predictive Maintenance

```
Incidents prevented (est. 40% of current incidents):
= 5 machines × 6 incidents × 40% = 12 incidents prevented
Cost saved = 12 × EUR 800 = EUR 9,600 / year

Unplanned downtime prevented (est. 4h per prevented incident):
= 12 incidents × 4h × EUR 120/h = EUR 5,760 / year
Total maintenance savings = EUR 15,360 / year
```
