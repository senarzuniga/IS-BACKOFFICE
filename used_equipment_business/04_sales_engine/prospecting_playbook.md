# Prospecting Playbook (Used Equipment)

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Company type: corrugated converter, fruit packer, FFG operator
- Revenue band: SME to mid-market with active CAPEX constraints
- Trigger signals:
  - New plant/capacity expansion
  - Line downtime or quality issues
  - Closure/divestment/asset sale nearby
  - Hiring for production scale-up

## Outreach Sequence (14 days)
1. Day 1: email with 2 relevant machine options and indicative ranges
2. Day 3: phone call to operations/plant manager
3. Day 5: LinkedIn message with case reference
4. Day 8: send technical checklist and financing alternatives
5. Day 11: offer 20-minute discovery call with technical advisor
6. Day 14: final follow-up with urgency window and next steps

## Qualification (BANT + Technical)
- Budget: CAPEX/OPEX and financing constraints
- Authority: decision maker and technical approver
- Need: throughput, quality, labor, maintenance pain
- Timeline: target commissioning date
- Technical: formats, speed, substrate, footprint, utilities

## Opportunity Stages
- S0 Signal detected
- S1 Contact validated
- S2 Discovery completed
- S3 Technical fit confirmed
- S4 Commercial proposal
- S5 Negotiation
- S6 Closed won/lost
