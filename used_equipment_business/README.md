# Used Equipment Trading Company - Operating Workspace

This workspace is dedicated to the used machinery trading business and is separated from fair/event intelligence.

## Goal
Build a repeatable business model to buy/sell used equipment for:
- 3 fruit packaging lines
- 1 flexo folder gluer (FFG)

## Structure
- 01_strategy: business model, positioning, economics
- 02_market_map: competitors, brokers, channels, geographies
- 03_offers: what to sell, pricing logic, commercial packages
- 04_sales_engine: ICP, outreach, qualification, pipeline
- 05_operations: asset validation, refurbishment, logistics, contracts
- 06_brand_and_website: website starter and messaging
- 07_templates: reusable templates for outreach and due diligence
- 08_kpis: scorecards and weekly dashboards

## First 30-day execution priorities
1. Sign 5 broker/dealer relationships (Oversys, Alpine Machinery, Papertek + 2 marketplaces).
2. Publish first 10 machine listings with complete technical dossiers.
3. Build first 100-account target list in EU + NA corrugated/fruit packaging segment.
4. Execute 3-channel outreach (email, phone, LinkedIn) with weekly KPI review.
5. Close 2 pilot deals (1 fruit line, 1 FFG or adjacent converting asset).
