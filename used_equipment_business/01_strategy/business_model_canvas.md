# Business Model Canvas - Used Equipment Trading

## Customer Segments
- Corrugated converters (SME/mid-size)
- Fruit and produce packers
- Folding carton and FFG plants
- Brokers and regional distributors

## Value Proposition
- Faster access to installed, proven equipment
- Lower CAPEX than new machinery
- Turnkey package: machine + logistics + commissioning + training
- Transparent technical condition and refurbishment plan

## Channels
- Direct outbound to plant managers and operations directors
- Partner dealers (Oversys, Alpine Machinery, Papertek)
- Marketplaces (Exapro, Kitmondo, Machineseeker, Machinio)
- Trade/association ecosystems for account discovery

## Revenue Streams
- Margin on machine buy/sell
- Refurbishment and retrofitting services
- Commission from brokerage-only deals
- Installation/commissioning service fees
- Spare parts and after-sales support

## Key Activities
- Sourcing and qualification of assets
- Technical due diligence and valuation
- Cross-border logistics and customs management
- Prospecting, negotiation, and closing

## Key Risks
- Hidden defects and downtime risk
- Logistics delays and freight cost volatility
- Low-quality leads and long sales cycles
- Price opacity in fragmented markets

## Mitigation
- Standardized inspection checklist
- SLA with logistics partners
- Lead scoring model and stage gates
- Benchmark pricing from 4+ channels
