# Website Plan - Used Equipment Trading

## Primary objective
Generate qualified inbound leads for used fruit packaging lines and used FFG assets.

## Core pages
- Home: positioning + trust + CTA
- Inventory: searchable machine listings
- Services: sourcing, inspection, logistics, commissioning
- Sell your machine: intake form for sellers
- Buyers: request brief form (required specs)
- About: technical credibility and process
- Contact: multi-channel CTA (WhatsApp/email/phone)

## Conversion blocks
- "Get machine shortlist in 48h"
- "Request valuation for your used machine"
- "Book 20-min technical call"

## Trust elements
- Inspection checklist preview
- Recent placements (anonymized if needed)
- Country coverage map
- Response SLA and process timeline
