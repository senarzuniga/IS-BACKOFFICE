# Deal Execution Checklist

## 1) Asset Validation
- Confirm serial number and model year
- Collect maintenance records
- Verify running hours and major replacements
- Video evidence of machine in operation
- Independent inspection report

## 2) Commercial Controls
- Define Incoterms and delivery scope
- Payment milestones with acceptance criteria
- Warranty/refund clauses and exclusions
- Spare parts availability and lead times

## 3) Logistics and Installation
- Dismantling plan and packing specification
- Transport route and customs documentation
- Site readiness checklist (power, air, space)
- Installation plan with commissioning protocol

## 4) Handover
- SAT/FAT equivalent acceptance report
- Operator training and SOP delivery
- First 30-day performance review
