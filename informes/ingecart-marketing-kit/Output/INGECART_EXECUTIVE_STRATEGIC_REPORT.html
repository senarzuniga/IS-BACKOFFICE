Executive Summary

Ingecart S.L. is an engineering & automation specialist for the paper and corrugated packaging industry with a clear focus on production optimisation, automation, and sustainability. Evidence in the repository shows a strong product set (Ingetrans, SR1400, portabobinas) and active go-to-market activity around trade fairs (FESPA, SuperCorr) and technology partnerships (IAR, MTorres).
