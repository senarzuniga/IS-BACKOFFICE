## Ejemplo de formato:

### Tema: Verb to be

1. Completa:
   - I ___ a student.
   - She ___ happy.
   - ___ you at school?

2. Elige la opción correcta:
   - He (is / are) my friend.
   - They (am / are) at home.

3. Corrige el error:
   - She are my sister. → ______

4. Traduce:
   - Yo soy profesor. → ______
